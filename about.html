Common Exercises

The following are common exercises that we encourage our clients to do as part of their daily exercise routine.

Burpee
Burpees are a great, full body excersise to increase your streignth and endurance. begin in a standing position,drop into a squat and extend forward again quickly, and then jump up from a squatted position
Plank
Planks build your core strength, To perform a plank, get in a pushup position and rest your forearms on the floor. hold the position as long as you can.
Mountain Climbers
Moutain Climbers are a good cario excercise. place your hands on the floor in a pushup position, then bring one leg up to your chest and the switch as quickly as you can. (As though your climbing a Mountain).
